Vision • Philippines • 2014

Automatic Visual Machine Inspection (AVMI)

Automated visual inspection replacing manual quality checks in high-volume electronics manufacturing.

Keyence PLCKeyence HMIVision inspectionSolidWorksIndustrial sensorsPrecision positioning
OverviewResponsibilitiesChallengeSolutionImpactLessons

Overview

Engineering context

Designed and developed a fully automated visual inspection system integrating industrial sensors, precision positioning, vision technology and PLC-controlled automation.

Responsibilities

My contribution

Machine concept development, mechanical design, PLC programming, HMI development, vision integration, sensor integration and electrical design support.

Challenge

What needed to be solved

Manual inspection depended heavily on operator experience, creating inconsistent quality, lower efficiency and higher risk of human error.

Solution

Engineering approach

Implemented synchronized product detection, automated inspection, pass/fail decision-making and production monitoring while maintaining cycle time.

Impact

Project outcomes

  • Improved inspection consistency
  • Reduced human error
  • Improved traceability
  • Reduced manual intervention

Lessons Learned

Engineering reflection

This project reinforced the importance of validating process requirements before finalizing the machine concept, involving production and maintenance teams early, and confirming performance through structured testing before handover.
