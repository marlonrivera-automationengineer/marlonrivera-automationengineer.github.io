Auto Laser Welding System

Precision automated laser welding supporting approximately 50,000 units per line per month.

OverviewResponsibilitiesChallengeSolutionImpactLessons

Overview

Engineering context

Developed a fully automated laser welding system for high-volume electronics manufacturing with precision motion and repeatable welding control.

Responsibilities

My contribution

Machine concept, mechanical design, PLC programming, servo motion integration, pneumatic design, machine assembly and production support.

Challenge

What needed to be solved

Maintain stable cycle time, repeatable positioning and consistent weld quality during continuous production.

Solution

Engineering approach

Synchronized positioning, laser activation, safety interlocks and diagnostics, followed by production validation and optimization.

Impact

Project outcomes

  • Approx. 50,000 units/line/month
  • Improved weld consistency
  • Reduced manual handling
  • Stable high-volume production

Lessons Learned

Engineering reflection

This project reinforced the importance of validating process requirements before finalizing the machine concept, involving production and maintenance teams early, and confirming performance through structured testing before handover.
